Student Questions

Sarah M.

Intermediate Level • 2 hours ago

"Why did tech stocks drop when the Fed raised rates? I thought higher rates were good for the economy?"

Expert Answer: Great question! Higher rates increase the cost of borrowing, which affects growth companies (like tech) more than value companies. Tech stocks are valued on future earnings, and higher discount rates make those future profits worth less today.
